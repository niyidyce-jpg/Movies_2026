Top 10 Movies of all TIME

Best Movies According To Malik

1. God Father

Directed by Francis Ford Coppola — often

considered the gold standard for crime dramas

2. Citizen kane

Revolutionary filmmaking by Orson Welles.

3. The Shawshank Redemption

A deeply emotional story of hope and friendship.

4. Schindler's List

Steven Spielberg’s powerful Holocaust drama.

5. The Dark Knight

Elevated superhero films into serious cinema, with Heath Ledger’s iconic Joker performance.

6. pulp friction

Quentin Tarantino’s nonlinear masterpiece

7. Casablanca

Classic romance and wartime drama with timeless dialogue.

8. 2001: A Space Odyssey

Stanley Kubrick’s visually groundbreaking sci-fi epic.

9. The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King

An epic fantasy achievement that swept the Oscars.

10. Seven Samurai

Akira Kurosawa’s hugely influential action drama.
